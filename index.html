Megha Multipurpose Training Institute Pvt. Ltd. is a Vocational Training Institute situated in Garuda, Bairiya-9 Rautahat and register under Company registration act and also under Cottage and Small Industry Office, Rautahat. MMPTI is also registered under VAT in Internal Land Revenue Office, Simara. MMPTI providing training in diverse trades since last nine years in different parts of Nepal. MMPTI is well equipped with training facilities, logistics, equipment, training materials and bigger pool of trainers. It has good track record of experience in providing trainings in diverse trades, provides better employment linkage with INGOs, NGOs, Consulting Firms, Contractor Firms, Industries, Business Group and Overseas Employment Companies. MMPTI has been affiliated to CTEVT as well.
